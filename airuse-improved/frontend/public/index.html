FAQ

Questions, answered

Is ResumeAI really free? ▾

Yes — generating, scoring, and downloading resumes is free with no credit card required. An optional free account lets you save resumes to a dashboard.

What is an ATS and why does my score matter? ▾

An Applicant Tracking System is software that screens resumes before a recruiter sees them. If your resume doesn't contain the right keywords in a parseable format, it's auto-rejected. A 90+ score means you clear that gate.

Will the AI make things up about my experience? ▾

No. ResumeAI rewrites and restructures only what you provide — it optimizes wording, ordering, and keyword placement, but the facts stay yours. Always review the output before applying.

What file formats can I download? ▾

Print-ready PDF, editable Word (.docx), and a plain-text ATS version that achieves the highest parse rate on strict job portals like Workday and Taleo.

Is my data safe? ▾

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored encrypted with Supabase. We never sell or share it, and you can delete your resumes — or your whole account — anytime.
